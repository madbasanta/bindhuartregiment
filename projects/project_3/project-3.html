Four artists from the same ethnic cultures and the remaining from different poles.

This collaboration is engrossing. Four artists from the same ethnic cultures and the remaining from different poles.The exchange of cultural knowledge has been up most priority to be focused during collaboration. Artists will work to convey proper grasp about Multilingual poetry penned by Milson.

The enlightening knowledge that our culture holds is something not be missed. "Sihe"commonly known as untimely/unwanted death has diffrent conspiring theory in diverse ethnic cultures of Nation.

This collaboration will give distinct insights to all artists about "Kirat mundum". Subtle beauty to Multilingual poetry "wahuima" has many insights to offer regarding kirat mundum: The whole endeavour of this collaboration was to jumble different sounds towards literature.To blend with ethnic cultural fusion band, performance art and dispense new methodology to transport poetry among unheard.

The workshop will be conducted for five days in the NATIONAL FOLK MUSIC MUSEUM tripureshwor. The core theme of the workshop is to help artists mix different textures of tribal instruments, poetry and sketches, theatrical acts into one place and practice the culture of exchange.

Selected artists:::::

Stotram स्तोत्रम् - Stotram is a ethnic cultural fusion band formed in Gorakhnath, Kathmandu, Nepal in 2020. Their music is based on Mantras and Stories, ethno- folk songs, world music and indigenous spiritual quotes fused with traditional musical instruments and modern western musical instruments

Subash poudel : Subash Poudel is a Nepal Based guitarist, music producer,and bassist of the indie folk fusion group Wayam and Bassist of Band Urjaa and White. His skillset includes music composing, arranging, producing, recording, editing, mixing, mastering and recording spot sound for movie and live sessions. His band Wayam is Planning to come up with an album in mid of 2022 with eight songs in total.

Sara Tunich Koinch : Sara is kathmandu based visual artist. She has participated in different workshops like Botanical illustrations 2014; Save elephant, save the earth organized by Apple international education 2016; The Object artist and the project vs subject creativity by (Theory and praxis with nexus culture) 2017

Dhe-Raaz magar : Dhe raaz is a theatrical performer who has been engaged with sarwanam theater since 2018. Milson rai : Milson Rai, he's the winner of All Nepal Poetry '2019' and has been writing poetry in free verse (गद्य), and classical metric verse (पद्य), & also a unique form of poetry known as Champu (चम्पू)
