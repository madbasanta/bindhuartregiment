Fill the space...

meet and greet event Jhapa.

We have come to jhapa to meet and discuss, share and receive collective insights on how we can together work to create more engaging projects on jhapa ! Let's talk about our communities, our environment, our culture, our vision overall let's talk about possibilities we can together create for everyone of us.

This project aims to use the medium of collaboration as a Methodology to celebrate, promote and inspire art as part of our life and celebration . By combining cultural exchange, research, workshops, public lectures we believe that this project will help us reflect on our culture, society and ourselves. We believe that this project will focus on artists who have not received the necessary network and attention to advance in their artistic journey/career.

Fill the space demonstrates the creative potential of artists to inspire social change, establish important public dialogue on key issues of society we live in and also reflect ourselves. Fill the space project will promote Eastern art space, collaboration between artists, introspection, positivity in the general community and promote Eastern culture ( support cultural dignity).

During the project we will have different intellectual discourses with expertise to increase the knowledge of our cultural importance and dignity. Unite artists together working and impacting at jhapa- Morang for future collaboration to showcase our regional art, culture and society to more audiences which can invite more researchers and artists to our region. We also aim to educate the younger generation to inspire change in our society and be the tool of transformation to deconstruct unhealthy narratives in society using art as a medium. We want to bring local stakeholders in connection with young artists to create healthy discussion / Space for critical discussion.

The customary problem artists outside the Kathmandu Valley encounter are not receiving needed attention for their work. How can we instill the idea of art and its importance in the general public. As artists, how are we creating an impact on our own community, which promotes the notion of artistic practice as more ethical and equally important as any other profession?" How can we use art practices as a reflection of our society, or expression of our lives , preservation of culture and constant reflection of us. We believe art is a transformative medium and equally powerful to change the unfortunate dogmas of society. Art is not just entertaining but enlightening ourselves with different perspectives and knowledge. FILL THE SPACE will create supportive ambeince for all senior artists of jhapa-morang to come along with the practicing and emerging artists united to tell our stories in future to a larger audience. To celebrate the uniqueness and cherish the practice of art as a whole.

सांस्कृतिक आदानप्रदान, अनुसन्धान, कार्यशालाहरू, सार्वजनिक व्याख्यानहरू संयोजन गरेर हामी विश्वास गर्छौं कि यो परियोजनाले हामीलाई हाम्रो संस्कृति, समाज र हामीलाई प्रतिबिम्बित गर्न मद्दत गर्नेछ। यस परियोजनाले आफ्नो कलात्मक यात्रा/पेशेमा अगाडी बढ्न आवश्यक सञ्जाल र ध्यान नपाएको कलाकारमा केन्द्रित हुने विश्वास लिएका छौं । “फिल द स्पेस” कार्यक्रमले झापा-मोरङमा कार्यरत र प्रभाव पार्ने सबै अग्रज वरिष्ठ कलाकारहरू अभ्यास र उदीयमान कलाकारहरू सँगै एकजुट भएर भविष्यमा यहाँको कलाकृति धेरै दर्शकहरु माझ पुर्याउन लागि सहयोगी वातावरण सिर्जना गर्दछ। “फिल द स्पेस” कार्यक्रमले विशिष्टता मनाउन ,समग्र रूपमा कलाको अभ्यासको कदर गर्न, कलाको प्रयोग मार्फत हाम्रो भाषा,संस्कृती ,पहिचानको जगेर्ना गर्न साथै समाज मा चेतना को दियो बल्न प्रेरित गर्ने छ।

“फिल द स्पेस “ कार्यक्रमले कलालाई हाम्रो जीवन र उत्सवको एक हिस्साको रूपमा मनाउन, प्रवर्द्धन गर्न र प्रेरणा दिन कलालाई नयाँ विधिको रूपमा प्रयोग गर्ने लक्ष्य राखेको छ। उक्त कार्यक्रमले कला को मार्फत सामाजिक परिवर्तनलाई प्रेरित गर्ने, हामी बस्ने समाजका मुख्य मुद्दाहरूमा एउटा महत्त्वपूर्ण सार्वजनिक संवाद स्थापना गर्ने र आफूलाई प्रतिबिम्बित गर्न कलाकारहरूको रचनात्मक क्षमता देखाउँछ। परियोजनाले पूर्वी ठाउँको कला, सन्स्कृती अनि सीमान्तकृत समुदायहरू जगेर्ना गर्ने / कलाकारहरू बीचको सहकार्य, आत्म निरीक्षण र सकारात्मकतालाई बढावा दिनेछ। हामी कसरी कलाको विचार र यसको महत्त्वलाई आम जनतामा जगाउन सक्छौं, कलाकारको हैसियतमा, हामी कसरी हाम्रो आफ्नै समुदायमा प्रभाव सिर्जना गर्दैछौं, जसले कलात्मक अभ्यासको धारणालाई अरू कुनै पेशा जत्तिकै नैतिक र उत्तिकै महत्त्वपूर्ण रूपमा बढावा दिन्छ?" हामी कसरी कला अभ्यासहरूलाई हाम्रो समाजको प्रतिविम्बको रूपमा प्रयोग गर्न सक्छौं, वा हाम्रो अभिव्यक्तिको रूपमा? जीवन, संस्कृतिको संरक्षण र हाम्रो निरन्तर प्रतिबिम्ब। हामी विश्वास गर्छौं कि कला एक परिवर्तनकारी माध्यम हो र समाजको दुर्भाग्यपूर्ण सिद्धान्तहरू परिवर्तन गर्न उत्तिकै शक्तिशाली हो। कलाले मनोरञ्जन मात्र होइन तर विभिन्न दृष्टिकोण र ज्ञानले आफूलाई प्रबुद्ध बनाउँछ।
