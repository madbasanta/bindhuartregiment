Poets and their
internal
complexities

Why are the concept of
some poet complex to
audience ?

A lot of time the idea of poets are hard to understand.There lies different element that artist have entangled on. The reason you see in modern art is there lies some internal complexities that lies within their soul ;which perhaps is only the path to reach an internal existence's abstract layer that poet wants to reach. The murmuration of characters a poet live by are not the scripted writing that could be filmed. " I want to kill a poet inside you" and unfortunately something else get vanished within the poet. The violence happens . There's so much of it inside a man that he almost can throw his head to the ground from the unreachable height and claim I'm not the mad man.

This war is not between poet and his internal demon but it is between something else that shapes him and his demon. This acts as an antagonist which help artist to write their pointed part.There is man and there is his co-rival in terms of suffering or credence of consequences which makes him paint what he do and it is not easy to crack the layers he writes.

When a poet get back to new timeline with same old cigarette or same old book or same old swollen days he hold yesterday , months , years or a second ago he tries not to build the co-domain between the “time and him ”vs“he and he”. This is not about the dream an artist lies at a point but is about the truth of his/her/theirs own unreachable light that sleeps somewhere beside them. Maybe their existence won't matter to reader’s time but then their existence matters to somebody else's time at the same horizon. They shall tell now that what flows within their head as a concept or any idea which might not go along with what shall flow in front of an audience the next few moments later. Putting it in a quick words these complexity on some specified artist is just like they thinking about burning the candle knowing the wax is all over them. The audience are always a part of candle.

Poojan Gyawali
3/7/2023
