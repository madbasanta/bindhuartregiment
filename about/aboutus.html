Welcome to Bindhu Art Regiment's "About Us" section. At the heart of this creative journey stand two passionate visionaries: Saugat Upreti and Poojan Gyawali. With unwavering dedication, they embarked on building the prototype of Bindhu Art Regiment years ago. As a harmonious team, they have nurtured the core of art and steered the realization of Bindhu Art Regiment's goals. Their collaborative spirit continues to shape our artistic community, inspiring artists to transcend boundaries and celebrate the boundless power of creativity.

Saugat Upreti

Founder / Curator

Saugat upreti is the founder of Bindhu art regiment. Lifelong creative with deep roots in the arts, film , music, literature and culture. He hosts the podcast under the pseudonym "le.laa" a moniker he discovered on his journey from Me to Mine. Moving from the eastern part of Nepal to the capital city Kathmandu his abilities to create critical discussion through art evolved from personal to collectives and joint works to create impact. As a freelancer he has worked on National and international art collaboration projects managing and assisting art projects of a diverse medium. Before the Bindhu art regiment he developed two other projects BREAKING SILENCE AND PROJECT NAADHE where he organised and curated many events and supported a new generation of artists to create and showcase their art works, music, writings and films.

Poojan Gyawali

Editor in Chief

Poojan Gyawali is an editor in Chief of Bindhu Art Regiment, a dynamic poet, and art practitioner. With an innate ability to infuse abstract elements into poetry, his writings weave emotions and narratives that resonate with the soul. As the Editor in Chief, Poojan plays a vital role in managing the operations of Bindhu Art Regiment, ensuring that our artistic endeavors and vision are brought to life through thoughtful curation and editorial excellence. Beyond poetry, Poojan is also a designer.His visionary ideas and understanding of the art world make him an invaluable asset to Bindhu Art Regiment. With Poojan at the helm, our artistic exploration knows no bounds, and the essence of creativity finds its home in the heart of Bindhu Art Regiment.
